Required Fields Summary

The following fields are required to create a product:

  • Product Name - A descriptive name for your product
  • SKU - Unique identifier
  • Category - Product category
  • Regular Price - Original selling price
  • Cost Price - Your purchase cost
  • Stock Quantity - Available inventory
  • Low Stock Threshold - Alert level
  • Short Description - Brief product overview
Images and specifications can be added later after product creation.
Quick Tips
  • Product Name

    Use descriptive names that include key features and specifications.

  • Pricing Strategy

    Set discount prices at least 10-15% below regular price for better conversions.

  • Product Images

    Use high-quality images (at least 800x800px) with consistent backgrounds.

  • Product Description

    Highlight key benefits and features in the first paragraph.

  • SEO Optimization

    Include relevant keywords in product name and description for better search ranking.
